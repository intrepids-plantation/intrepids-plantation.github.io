About Us

Intrepids Plantations is a Kenyan-based business specializing in the supply of high-quality electricity poles, fencing poles, and expert installation services.

Our mission is to support rural and urban infrastructure by offering reliable, affordable, and durable pole solutions. Whether you're building power lines, securing farmland, or fencing residential property — we’ve got you covered.

We combine skilled workmanship with trusted materials to ensure every installation meets safety standards and customer expectations.
